Our History

Treasure-Home School was established with a vision to provide sound education in a nurturing environment. Since its founding, the school has grown steadily with the goal of raising academically excellent and morally upright students.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence where students are inspired to reach their full potential and become responsible leaders of tomorrow.

Our Mission

To deliver quality education through dedicated teachers, disciplined learning, and strong moral values, preparing every child for success in life.

  • Godliness
